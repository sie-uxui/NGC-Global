## Product Categories & Details

### 1. **Dredger Gearbox Systems**

#### Submerged Cutter Gearbox
- **Application:** Cutter suction dredgers (CSD)
- **Features:** 
  - Deep water operation (max depth ~30m)
  - High sand prevention and sealing requirements
  - Low RPM, super large torque
  - Large ahead and astern pull
  - Maximum work angle: 60-65°
- **Notable Specs:**
  - Power: 2×2,600 kW (dual input, single output)
  - Maximum output torque: 1800 kN·m
  - Weight: Up to 220T for largest models
  - Used in 3,500 - 6,500 m³/h dredgers

#### Dredge Pump Gearbox (Cabin Type)
- **Application:** In-cabin dredge pump systems
- **Notable Projects:**
  - 8000m³ hopper dredger configurations
  - Double ratio gearbox: 3300kW input, 600rpm input speed
  - Output speeds: 340rpm (pump off), 220rpm (stowing)

#### Submerged Dredge Pump Gearbox
- **Application:** Underwater pump driving for CSD and bucket wheel dredgers
- **Features:**
  - Big variation of sand and water density tolerance
  - Deep water operation (around 30m)
  - High sand prevention and sealing requirements
  - Super large torque and large pull
- **Specs:**
  - Input power: Up to 3700kW
  - Long shaft line configuration available

#### Dredge Pump & Gearbox Integrated System
- **Types:** Horizontal and vertical sections
- **Specs:**
  - Input power: 2×1400kW - 3700kW
  - Applies for 4500m³ - 8000m³ dredgers

#### High Pressure Water Pump Gearbox
- **Application:** Hopper dredgers
- **Specs:**
  - Input power: 1200kW
  - Input speed: 1500rpm
  - Output speed: 458rpm
  - Applies for 11,000m³ hopper dredgers

#### Hydraulic Power Pack Gearbox
- **Application:** Piling barges and large cutter dredgers
- **Features:** Drive multi-pumps, high output speed
- **Configuration:** Single input, 6 outputs available
- **Specs:**
  - Input power: 1470kW
  - Input speed: 600rpm
  - Output speed: 1800rpm

#### Bucket Wheel Gearbox
- **Application:** Bucket wheel dredgers
- **Features:** Specialized for rotating bucket wheel mechanisms

#### Pivot Shaft Gearbox
- **Application:** Dredger positioning systems

#### Side Winch Gearbox
- **Type:** MJ100_003 series
- **Specs:**
  - Weight: 60T per unit
  - Maximum output torque: 2000 kN·m
  - ANSYS-optimized housing
  - Low noise, low vibration
  - Electric motor pump-driven lubrication

### 2. **Marine Propulsion Systems**

#### NCP Series - Controllable Pitch Propeller (CPP)
- Full CPP system integration
- Compatible with hybrid power systems

#### CK Series - CPP Gearbox
- **Features:**
  - Speed reduction, clutch-in/off, PTO and PTI functions
  - For non-reversible medium speed diesel engines
- **Types:** CKV, CKH, CKTS series
- **Specs:**
  - Max transmission power: 17,000kW
  - Max transmission capacity: 50kW/rpm
  - Max thrust: 1850kN
  - Gear material: High quality low carbon alloy steel/carburizing and quenching
  - Hydraulic multi-disc friction clutch

#### CG Series - Fixed Pitch Propeller Gearbox
- **Functions:** Astern-ahead, clutch-in/off, speed reduction
- **Application:** FPP systems with reversible medium speed diesel engines
- **Types:** Six structure variants (CGC, CGS, CGD, CGH, CGL, CGK)
- **Transmission:** One or double stage
- **Shaft arrangements:** Coaxial, horizontal, vertical, non-concentric

#### Main Propulsion Gearbox
- **Recent Achievement:** 38,168 cubic meters TSHD (largest in Asia, third globally)
- **Configuration:** Single-input, dual-output vertical arrangement
- **Integration:** Twin-engine, twin-propeller systems
- **Partners:** Wartsila packaged systems
- **Power:** 14,400 kW per engine at 600rpm

#### NRP Series - Azimuth Thruster (FP/CP)
- Fixed and controllable pitch configurations
- Multiple configuration options available

#### NEPod Series - Electrical Propulsion System
- Modern electric propulsion solutions

#### NFT/NCT Series - Tunnel Thruster
- Bow and stern thruster systems

#### NFP Series - Shaft/Rudder System
- Complete propulsion shaft and steering solutions

#### NRV Series - Rotary Vane Steering Gear
- Marine steering systems

#### Jacking Gears
- Auxiliary turning gear systems

#### NHF/NHC Series - Hydraulic Coupling
- Power transmission coupling systems
